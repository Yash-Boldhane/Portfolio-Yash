Chat Bot

A chatbot is a software program designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the internet. It's a form of artificial intelligence (AI) that interprets and processes user inputs (typically text-based) and generates responses accordingly. Here's a basic breakdown of how chatbots work: Input Analysis: When a user sends a message or query to the chatbot, the chatbot analyzes the text to understand its intent and extract relevant information. This process often involves natural language understanding (NLU) techniques to comprehend the meaning behind the user's words. Processing and Context: Once the input is understood, the chatbot processes it and determines an appropriate response.
